Ebola outbreak response

http://ehealthafrica.org

We provide on-going consultation on Ebola surveillance and response platforms.

In continuing collaboration with eHealth Africa, we released Sense; an award-winning suite of open source tools to support emergency management staff in tracing people who had been in contact with those infected by Ebola.

Sense Followup — an offline-first mobile app — helps cut healthcare response time from days to minutes, contributing to containing the outbreak in Nigeria and continues to save lives in West Africa.
