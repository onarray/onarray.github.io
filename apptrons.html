Apptrons

Apptrons is a multi-lingual e-commerce website.

In collaboration with WholeDayBuy, a Hong Kong-based deal-of-the-day and group buying startup, onarray produced a Laravel-powered, muli-lingual e-commerce website to showcase their latest products and deals.

Catering for comprehensive requirements, we built apptrons to be scalable from the ground up; each function of the website is an isolated component, brought together with a powerful administration panel. Consisting of over 18 components, ranging from content management to social integration, apptrons is highly robust and extensible.
